A discription of each pack included in the base download

-AntiBio
Adds a set of various anti-bio weapons, including an Anti-Bio Beam Mark 2, an Anti-Bio Bomb and an Anti-Bio Laser.

-ChainCharge
Expands the selection of chain weapons and charge weapons in the game.

-Crystals
Adds more crystal weapons

-Deceit
Clones certain weapons but changes their animation to be of another weapon. If you don't pay attention, you could end up conned.

-Hidden
Hides weapon information, now you won't know what changed!

-Pulsers
Adds a new weapon, an ion weapon that can pierce shields, causing extra ion-damage to any shields it passes through.
